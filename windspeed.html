Wind Speed

Windspeed proves itself to be the least interesting dataset of the bunch, with a graph that shows the relatively low winds across the board within the majority of cities sampled. It is logical for more extreme winds to be found further away from the equator. As it is shown, the highest windspeeds are found furthest away from the equator.
